Fleet Bees

Fleet Bees is an advanced Transport Management System integrated with SAP Business One, designed to streamline logistics, improve delivery accuracy, and boost operational visibility. With features like route optimization, live GPS tracking, driver mobile apps, and 3PL integration, it delivers real-time logistics intelligence for modern enterprises.

Key Features

  • Route Optimization

  • AI-powered route planning based on distance, traffic, and delivery priority

  • Minimize fuel costs and delays with dynamic routing

  • Real-time re-routing based on live conditions

  • GPS-based Live Vehicle Tracking

  • Real-time location of fleet on an interactive map

  • View trip progress, speed, and stop alerts

  • Integrated with SAP delivery and shipping modules

  • Driver Mobile Application

  • Android/iOS app for delivery task management

  • Load verification with barcode/RFID scan

  • Digital proof of delivery and customer signature capture

  • Shipping and Customer Acceptance

  • Auto-update SAP B1 with delivery status

  • Capture customer feedback and delivery issues

  • Real-time shipment notifications

  • 3PL and Carrier Integration

  • Integration with third-party logistics APIs

  • Automated AWB generation and tracking

  • Multi-carrier dispatching and SLA monitoring

Use Cases

Distributors and wholesalers managing own or outsourced fleets

E-commerce and retail businesses with last-mile delivery

Manufacturers with inter-facility or vendor shipments

Logistics service providers offering B2B delivery solutions

Benefits at a Glance

  • Streamline fleet and logistics operations with SAP B1 integration
  • Improve on-time delivery rates with optimized routes and tracking
  • Enhance visibility and control across delivery lifecycle
  • Reduce delivery disputes with real-time proof of delivery
  • Boost customer satisfaction with accurate ETAs and updates
