Buzz Commerce

Buzz Commerce is a modern commerce suite integrated with SAP Business One, designed for businesses seeking a unified digital platform that connects e-commerce, customer engagement, payments, and POS in one intelligent solution.

Key Features

  • Real-Time SAP Business One Integration

  • Syncs products, customers, inventory, and orders

  • Real-time stock updates and order tracking

  • Automatic journal entries and invoicing in SAP

  • E-Commerce Web Application

  • Fully responsive storefront with customizable themes

  • SEO-friendly product pages, wish lists, reviews, and filtering

  • Multi-currency, multi-language, and tax rule support

  • Customer Self-Service Portal

  • View order history, invoices, and delivery status

  • Track returns and raise support tickets

  • B2B features: bulk orders, price lists, and credit limits

  • Payment Gateway Integration

  • Connects with Stripe, Razorpay, PayPal, Square, Authorize.Net, and more

  • Supports subscriptions, partial payments, refunds

  • PCI-DSS compliant and secure transactions

  • POS System Integration

  • Retail POS module connected directly with SAP B1

  • Barcode scanning, cash drawer, and receipt printing support

  • Offline mode with auto-sync when connected
